Steps

  1. mix the dry ingredients
  2. add butter to the dry ingredients and mix gradually untill crumbly texture achieved
  3. add water gradually and fold untill combined
  4. keep in refridgerator for 30min
  5. in a pot add your oil and onions
  6. once oil is translucent,add minced meat
  7. add the vegetables and seasoning
  8. allow to cook and set aside to cool before usage
  9. roll dough and add fillings
  10. bake for 20-25min untill golden
